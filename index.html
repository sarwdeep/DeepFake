DeepFake Detection

Deepfakes are a form of synthetic media that use advanced artificial intelligence (AI) techniques, particularly deep learning algorithms, to create convincing fake videos or images. These techniques are capable of manipulating or superimposing existing images and videos onto target faces or bodies, creating highly realistic but entirely fabricated content.
